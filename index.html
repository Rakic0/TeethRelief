How to use TeethRelief

  • Step 1: Brush your teeth properly before you start using the kit. Gently brush your teeth without hurting your gums.
  • Step 2: Check if the batteries are in the LED light and fasten it to the mouth tray.
  • Step 3: Apply the gel to the top and bottom parts of the tray and place it in your mouth.
  • Step 4: Get it in, press the button, and whiten your teeth for 10 minutes. Having an in-built timer will turn off the light automatically once the 10 minutes are over.
  • Step 5: Spit and wash after use. You have to repeat the same for six applications.
  • Step 6: Finally, you can take off the tray from your mouth and clean the gel out using warm water.
